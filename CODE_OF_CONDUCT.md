# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want gitsafe to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone,
regardless of experience level, background, or identity. We pledge to make
participation a respectful experience for all.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in language and actions.
- Welcome newcomers and assume good intent.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and community.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments, public or private.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Sexualized language or imagery, or unwelcome sexual attention.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any
communication done in the project's name — and when representing the project in
public spaces.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainer at **xmonader@gmail.com**. All
reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and the reporter's
privacy will be respected. Maintainers may take any action they deem
appropriate, from a warning to a permanent ban from the project.
